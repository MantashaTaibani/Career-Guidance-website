What is Arts (Humanities)? 🎭

Arts stream is for students interested in society, culture, history, and creativity. It helps you understand human behavior, communication, and social systems.

Subjects 📚

📖 History
🌍 Geography
🧠 Psychology
⚖️ Political Science
📚 Sociology
🎨 Fine Arts / Literature

Top Career Options 🚀

⚖️ Lawyer
📰 Journalist
🎓 Teacher / Professor
🧠 Psychologist
🎨 Designer / Artist
🏛️ Civil Services (IAS, IPS)

Career Roadmap 🛣️

10th → 11th Arts → 12th → BA → MA / Professional Course → Job 💼

Skills Required 💡

✔ Creativity
✔ Communication skills
✔ Critical thinking
✔ Writing skills

Did You Know? 🤔

💡 Arts students can go into law, media, design, and government jobs
💡 Civil services (IAS/IPS) is a top career choice for Arts students
