What is Science? 🔬

Science stream is for students interested in technology, medicine, research, and innovation. It helps you understand how the world works through experiments and logic.

Subjects 📚

⚛️ Physics
🧪 Chemistry
🧬 Biology
➕ Mathematics
💻 Computer Science

Top Career Options 🚀

👨‍⚕️ Doctor (MBBS)
👨‍💻 Engineer
🔬 Scientist
✈️ Pilot
💻 Software Developer
🧠 Data Scientist

Career Roadmap 🛣️

10th → 11th Science → 12th → B.Tech / MBBS / B.Sc → Higher Studies → Job 💼

Skills Required 💡

✔ Problem-solving skills
✔ Analytical thinking
✔ Curiosity & research mindset
✔ Logical reasoning

Did You Know? 🤔

💡 Science offers the highest number of career options
💡 You can switch to commerce or management after science
