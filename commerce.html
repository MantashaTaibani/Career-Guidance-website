What is Commerce? 💼

Commerce is the stream for students interested in business, finance, and management. It helps you understand how money, companies, and markets work.

Subjects 📚

📊 Accountancy
🏢 Business Studies
💰 Economics
➕ Mathematics (Optional)

Top Career Options 🚀

👔 Chartered Accountant (CA)
📜 Company Secretary (CS)
🎓 B.Com / BBA
🏦 Banking & Finance
💡 Entrepreneur

Career Roadmap 🛣️

10th → 11th Commerce → 12th → B.Com / BBA → MBA → Job 💼

Skills Required 💡

✔ Good with numbers
✔ Analytical thinking
✔ Communication skills

Did You Know? 🤔

💡 CA is one of the highest-paying careers in commerce
💡 Commerce students can go into IT & Data Science too
