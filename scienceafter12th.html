Science Stream Careers (PCM)

Engineering – B.Tech / B.E.

4-year degree in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and more.

4-year degree in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and 30+ specialisations. Top institutes: IITs, NITs, BITS Pilani. Duration: 4 Years | Exam: JEE Main / JEE Advanced | Avg Salary: Rs. 4 - 20 LPA

Commercial Pilot

Earn a CPL after 12th with Physics and Maths. High-paying aviation career.

Duration: 2-4 Years | Exam: DGCA CPL | Avg Salary: Rs. 8 - 20 LPA

Architecture – B.Arch

5-year program in building design and planning.

Entrance via NATA or JEE Paper 2. High demand in urban development. Duration: 5 Years | Exam: NATA / JEE Paper 2 | Avg Salary: Rs. 3 - 10 LPA

Science Stream Careers (PCB)

MBBS – Medical Doctor.

45.5-year medical degree for becoming a doctor. Entrance via NEET-UG. Top colleges: AIIMS Delhi, JIPMER, AFMC.

Duration: 5.5 Years | Exam: NEET-UG | Avg Salary: Rs. 6 - 25 LPA

Pharmacy – B.Pharm

4-year degree in pharmaceutical sciences. Excellent scope in R&D, hospitals and retail pharmacy.

Duration: 4 Years | Exam: State CET | Avg Salary: Rs. 3 - 8 LPA

Nursing – B.Sc Nursing

4-year program for hospital and community healthcare. High demand in India and abroad especially after COVID..

Duration: 4 Years | Exam: NEET / Merit | Avg Salary: Rs. 2.5 - 6 LPA
