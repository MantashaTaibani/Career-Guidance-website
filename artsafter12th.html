Arts / Humanities Stream Careers

Law – BA LLB / LLB

5-year integrated or 3-year LLB after graduation. Entrance via CLAT for National Law Universities. High-demand profession.
Duration: 5 Years | Exam: CLAT | Avg Salary: Rs. 4 - 15 LPA

IAS / IPS – Civil Services

Prestigious government career via UPSC. Open to graduates of any stream. Most respected and competitive exam in India.
Duration: Graduation + Preparation | Exam: UPSC CSE | Salary: Rs. 56,000 - 2,50,000/month

Journalism and Mass Communication

3-year degree in media, reporting, broadcasting and digital journalism. Growing field in online and social media.
Duration: 3 Years | Exam: Merit / Entrance | Avg Salary: Rs. 2.5 - 8 LPA
