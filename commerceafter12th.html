Commerce Stream Careers

Chartered Accountancy – CA
Professional course by ICAI. One of India's most prestigious qualifications in finance and auditing.
Duration: 4-5 Years | Exam: CA Foundation - Inter - Final | Avg Salary: Rs. 6 - 20 LPA

BBA + MBA – Management
3-year BBA followed by 2-year MBA. Gateway to management, marketing, HR and leadership roles.
Duration: 3 + 2 Years | Exam: CAT / MAT / XAT | Avg Salary: Rs. 5 - 30 LPA

Banking and Finance
B.Com plus CFA / CFP or government bank exams. Roles in PSU banks, RBI, SEBI and private sector finance.
Duration: 3 Years + Exam Prep | Exam: IBPS / SBI / RBI | Avg Salary: Rs. 3 - 10 LPA
